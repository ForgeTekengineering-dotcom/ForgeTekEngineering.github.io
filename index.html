How it works

  1. Kickoff: quick intake call + drawings/photos + deliverable list
  2. Draft: early draft package for review (so we’re not fixing it at the end)
  3. Finalize: revisions, issue PDFs, and provide editable files as agreed
  4. Support: redlines/as-builts and construction coordination if needed

What I need from you

Even rough info helps—send what you’ve got.

  • Existing drawings (SLD/MCC/panel) if available
  • Equipment list (motors, drives, instruments, panels)
  • Voltage levels and control power assumptions
  • Preferred standards/title blocks/layering (if any)
